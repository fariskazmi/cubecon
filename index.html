About Us

Cube Construction Ltd. was founded with the 21st century in mind. We are a forward thinking company that is heavily focused on the integration of modern technologies with today's traditional consultation.

Our Values

Every project is handled in the same manner regardless of size. We dedicate ourselves to satisfy each and every customer. We provide expert consultation from people with years of experience in the field. Quality is our priority in every project we manage.

What we do

We provide the following services and consultations:

  • Residential
  • Commercial
  • Special Projects

Residential

We consult on new single family, and multi family home construction. Our team is also experienced in home renovations and improvement.

Commercial

​We have the proper apparatus and experience to handle small scale commercial projects such as government facility renovations, office space renovations, tenants improvements and mechanical or electrical upgrades.

Special Projects

Our team of experts are specialized in the fields of sub-metering (power, water, energy) supply, install, programs, and commissioning of sub metering devices
